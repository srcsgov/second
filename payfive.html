Care & Maintenance

Cleaning: Wipe daily with a microfiber cloth to remove dirt and oils. For stainless steel or waterproof models, use mild soap, lukewarm water, and a soft brush. Dry with a lint-free cloth.

Water Resistance: Ensure the crown is fully screwed before water exposure. Rinse with fresh water after saltwater use. Do not operate the crown or pushers underwater.

Handling and Storage: Keep away from magnetic fields and extreme temperatures. Store in a dry, dedicated watch box when not in use.

Maintenance: Service every 5-10 years for optimal performance. Replace batteries promptly to prevent leaks. Use only authorized service centers.

Precautions: Avoid impacts or exposure to chemicals like perfumes and detergents to protect the case and strap.
